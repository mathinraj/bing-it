Why Bing it?

Every detail engineered to look natural and keep your sessions undetectable.

⌨

Human-like Typing

Characters typed one-by-one with variable delays (45–580ms), including occasional "thinking" pauses that mimic real behavior.

📜

Natural Scrolling

Scrolls search results in random increments with reading pauses to simulate genuine browsing of results.

☕

Coffee Breaks

Configurable long pauses between searches — just like a real person would take breaks while browsing.

📈

Google Trends

Pulls real-world trending queries so your search history looks 100% natural and topically relevant.

⏱

Floating Timer

Live countdown overlay on the Bing tab showing progress, current status, and time to next search.

⚙

Fully Configurable

Control delay ranges, break frequency, break duration, and reading overlap — all from a dedicated settings page.

Get started in seconds

1

Install the extension

Add it to Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome from the web store with a single click.

2

Sign in to Microsoft

Make sure you're signed into your Microsoft account in the browser.

3

Set your search count

Open the extension popup, pick how many searches you want (5–90+), and hit "Bing it!"

4

Sit back and relax

Searches run in a background tab. Keep browsing — you'll see the floating timer on the Bing tab when you check in.
